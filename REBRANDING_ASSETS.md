## Asset Creation Guidelines

### Color Palette Recommendations
```
Primary Colors:
- Electric Blue: #00D4FF, #0099FF
- Neon Cyan: #00FFFF, #00E5E5
- Deep Blue/Black: #001133, #000A1A

Accent Colors:
- Energy White: #FFFFFF, #F0F8FF
- Pulse Green: #00FF88 (optional)
- Warning Red: #FF0055 (for shields/damage)
```

### Typography
**Current Font:** Bebas Neue (display font)
- Consider keeping for consistency
- Or choose similar angular/futuristic font
- Examples: Orbitron, Exo 2, Audiowide, Michroma

   - Digital grid lines
   - Wireframe elements

2. **Pulse/Energy Theme:**
   - Glowing effects
   - Energy waves
   - Electric arcs
   - Animated pulses (for future)

3. **Futuristic Racing:**
   - Speed lines
   - Motion blur effects
   - High-tech overlays
